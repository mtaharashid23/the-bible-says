Value

We uphold the principles of truth, clarity, and accessibility, ensuring every scripture shared nurtures trust, deepens faith, and promotes a lifelong commitment to understanding and living God’s Word.

Vision

To inspire believers worldwide to cultivate a deep, personal connection with God by providing structured, accessible Biblical knowledge that strengthens faith, fosters understanding, and empowers everyday spiritual living.

About the Author

Raul Duran

Raul Duran, nearly a Southern California native, has spent most of his life near the ocean he loves, returning to the region with his family after a decade out of state. A devoted husband to his wife, Cindy, and father to four children two adult and two young Raul’s life is deeply rooted in faith and family. Raised and baptized as the son of a Mexican Methodist, he was immersed in church life from a young age, singing in the choir and attending Sunday school. A devastating fire that destroyed his childhood church disrupted this connection, leaving Raul without a place of worship for many years.

In his mid-twenties, Raul felt a profound calling to reconnect with his faith, inspired by his father’s example as a righteous man and the encouragement of a lifelong Christian friend. This led him to immerse himself in daily Bible study, culminating in a greater conscious decision to be re-baptized as an adult. Raul’s continued study of scripture has not only shaped his life but also inspired his writing, through which he shares his witness of God’s grace and blessings.

About the Book

The Bible Says

The Bible Says... by Raul Duran is a carefully organized collection of full scriptures categorized by vital subjects, designed to help readers quickly understand Biblical truths. Through contextual notes and historical insights, it provides a rapid foundation for spiritual growth, strengthening faith, and fostering a deeper relationship with God. It serves as a guide for new believers, seasoned Christians, and anyone seeking a more structured and accessible approach to scripture study.
